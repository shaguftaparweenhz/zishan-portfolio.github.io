About Me

I've spent 13 years in FAANG environments building Artificial Intelligence SaaS products relying heavily on Optimization and Machine Learning. During my 7 years at Amazon, I led scientists and engineers through the entire journey—from product discovery and vision to global launch and continuous enhancement.

I'm a hands-on leader who never just sits in meetings. I love rolling up my sleeves to work on mathematical modeling and coding right alongside my team. While I'm expert at managing Scrum/Jira and creating multi-year product roadmaps, my real strength lies in system orchestration—integrating multiple technical systems through APIs, microservices, and real-time data streams to drive business decisions.

As an AWS Certified Solutions Architect, I focus on system design and risk management to ensure products can scale globally. I believe in fast, iterative prototyping and A/B testing to validate complex system hypotheses against OKRs and core business KPIs.

Beyond work, I stay active in the tech community as a research collaborator for AI for Smart Mobility (IEEE) and a volunteer for Optimization4All, helping make decision intelligence education accessible to everyone.
